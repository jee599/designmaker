The Maison

Craft distilled to its essence.

Founded by master distiller Elena Kors in a converted greenhouse outside Oslo, Maison Noir began as a single experiment: what happens when you freeze fermentation at its most volatile moment?

Twelve years and four hundred iterations later, the answer lives in every bottle we release. Frost on emerald glass. A spirit that remembers winter.
