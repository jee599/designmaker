Questions from the audience

Behind the Scenes

How does versioning actually work?

Every save creates a frame in your reel. Frames are immutable — you can't edit a past frame, only create a new one. This means your history is always intact. Scrub back to any point, fork from any frame, compare any two frames side by side. Storage is delta-based, so 1,000 frames uses less space than you'd expect.

What file types are supported?

Images (PNG, JPG, SVG, WebP), documents (PDF, Markdown), design files (Figma links rendered as previews), and code snippets (syntax highlighted in 40+ languages). Video support is in beta — each video gets its own sub-reel.

Can clients view reels without an account?

Yes. Share a link with an optional expiry date and password. Clients can scrub through frames, leave comments pinned to specific frames, and approve directly. No signup, no download. Comments sync back to your workspace in real time.
