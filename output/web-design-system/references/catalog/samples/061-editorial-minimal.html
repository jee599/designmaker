FEATURES

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Six tools, one workspace. Each built to stay out of your way until you need it.

Documents

Write and nest pages without limits. Markdown shortcuts, tables, and embeds built in. No formatting toolbar clutter.

Projects

Kanban boards, timelines, and lists that adapt to your workflow. Switch views without losing context.

Search

Find anything across every page and project in under 200ms. Filters by author, date, and tag. No indexing delays.

Collaboration

Real-time editing with presence indicators. Comment threads that resolve into action items. No notification overload.

Set rules once and forget them. Move tasks on status change, assign reviewers on PR merge, archive after 30 days of inactivity.
